<p><b>Card-Jitsu cards</b> were the main feature of the <a href="/wiki/Card-Jitsu" title="Card-Jitsu">Card-Jitsu</a> series of games. They were special cards, found by <a href="/wiki/Sensei" title="Sensei">Sensei</a> long ago. There were 509 different kinds of cards in the game, with a variety of other cards that were not in the game, and only existed in real-life. Each card could be one of six colors, either red, blue, yellow, green, orange, or purple. Each card also had a value, which was a number ranging from 2 to 12. Lastly, cards each had an element, that determined which card would win. If the elements of two played cards were the same, the one with the higher value won. If both values were the same, the cards were tied.
</p><p>Card-Jitsu cards could be unlocked with codes from real life cards, and Power Cards could also be bought three at a time (one of each element) for 1500 <a href="/wiki/Coin" title="Coin">coins</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Martial_Artworks" title="Martial Artworks">Martial Artworks</a> catalog, as of the <a href="/wiki/Dojo" title="Dojo">Dojo</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Renovation" title="Renovation">renovation</a> in May 2013. Only <a href="/wiki/Member" class="mw-redirect" title="Member">members</a> could buy cards from Martial Artworks, and if a player's membership expired, the cards bought from Martial Artworks would not be able to be used until membership was renewed.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Real_Life_Variant">Real Life Variant</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a title="Sign in to edit" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" data-tracking-label="log-in-edit-section" data-action="edit-section" href="https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fclubpenguin.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FCard-Jitsu_Cards%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D4&amp;uselang=en" data-testid="log-in-edit-section"><svg class="wds-icon wds-icon-tiny"><use xlink:href="#wds-icons-pencil-tiny"></use></svg></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><i>Main article: <a href="/wiki/List_of_Card-Jitsu_sets" title="List of Card-Jitsu sets">List of Card-Jitsu sets</a></i>
</p><p>Power Cards were special types of cards that had special effects when played or scored. These could vary from removing scored cards from your opponent, or increasing the value of your own cards. Power Cards also had animations when played, most being unique, although a few Power Cards shared animations with others. As described by <a href="/wiki/Aunt_Arctic" title="Aunt Arctic">Aunt Arctic</a> in issue 163 of the <a href="/wiki/Club_Penguin_Times" title="Club Penguin Times">Club Penguin Times</a>, "They have the ability to completely alter the world around you, and summon powerful objects to help you win." Power Cards were surrounded by a glow in-game, or have holographic borders in real life.
</p>
